I'm both an industry professional at Pinnacle Sports as a Machine Learning Engineer and an academic, teaching at York University's School of Information Technology.

My research interest lies in the domain of machine learning models, with a specific focus on generative neural networks applied to image and graph structures. Furthermore, I utilize these machine learning models to encapsulate real-world scenarios, such as sports events, providing a computational perspective to understand and predict real-world dynamics.

As an avid explorer, I relish the opportunity to traverse our planet's diverse landscapes. From bustling cities to serene natural habitats, I cherish every experience. I often wander in nature, camping under the stars and feeling the world's pulse firsthand. My camera is a constant companion, capturing fleeting moments and turning them into lasting memories.

When I'm not on a physical journey, I embark on cultural odysseys through diverse media. As an avid reader, I let brilliant authors transport me across different dimensions, while films immerse me in a wide array of narratives and perspectives. Being a cinephile and a dedicated music enthusiast, I appreciate the profound impact of a compelling storyline and the rhythm that stirs the soul. These intellectual and sensory adventures continually enrich my perspective on life.
